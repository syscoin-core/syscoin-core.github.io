Arguments: 1. txid (string, required) A transaction that is in the block Result: "proof" (string) JSON representation of merkle proof (transaction index, siblings and block header and some other information useful for moving coins/assets to another chain) Examples: > syscoin-cli syscoingetspvproof dfc7eac24fa89b0226c64885f7bedaf132fc38e8980b5d446d76707027254490 > curl --user myusername --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id":"curltest", "method": "syscoingetspvproof", "params": [dfc7eac24fa89b0226c64885f7bedaf132fc38e8980b5d446d76707027254490] }' -H 'content-type: text/plain;' http://127.0.0.1:8370/
