--- name: mnsync btcversion: 4.1.2 btcgroup: governance permalink: en/doc/4.1.2/rpc/governance/mnsync/ --- mnsync "command" Returns the sync status, updates to the next step or resets it entirely. Arguments: 1. command (string, required) The command to issue (status|next|reset) Result: "result" (string) Result Examples: > syscoin-cli mnsync status > curl --user myusername --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id":"curltest", "method": "mnsync", "params": [status] }' -H 'content-type: text/plain;' http://127.0.0.1:8370/
