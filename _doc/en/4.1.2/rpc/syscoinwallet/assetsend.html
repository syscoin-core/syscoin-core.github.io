--- name: assetsend btcversion: 4.1.2 btcgroup: syscoinwallet permalink: en/doc/4.1.2/rpc/syscoinwallet/assetsend/ --- assetsend asset_guid "address" amount Send an asset you own to another address. Arguments: 1. asset_guid (numeric, required) The asset guid. 2. address (string, required) The address to send the asset to (creates an asset allocation). 3. amount (numeric or string, required) The quantity of asset to send. Result: { "hex": "hexstring" (string) the unsigned transaction hexstring. } Examples: > syscoin-cli assetsend "assetguid" "address" "amount" > curl --user myusername --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id":"curltest", "method": "assetsend", "params": ["assetguid", "address", "amount"] }' -H 'content-type: text/plain;' http://127.0.0.1:8370/
