--- name: syscoinburntoassetallocation btcversion: 4.1.2 btcgroup: syscoinwallet permalink: en/doc/4.1.2/rpc/syscoinwallet/syscoinburntoassetallocation/ --- syscoinburntoassetallocation "funding_address" asset_guid amount Burns Syscoin to the SYSX asset Arguments: 1. funding_address (string, required) Funding address to burn SYS from 2. asset_guid (numeric, required) Asset guid of SYSX 3. amount (numeric or string, required) Amount of SYS to burn. Result: { "hex": "hexstring" (string) the unsigned transaction hexstring. } Examples: > syscoin-cli syscoinburntoassetallocation "funding_address" "asset_guid" "amount" > curl --user myusername --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id":"curltest", "method": "syscoinburntoassetallocation", "params": ["funding_address", "asset_guid", "amount"] }' -H 'content-type: text/plain;' http://127.0.0.1:8370/
