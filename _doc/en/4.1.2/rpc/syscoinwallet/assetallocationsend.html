--- name: assetallocationsend btcversion: 4.1.2 btcgroup: syscoinwallet permalink: en/doc/4.1.2/rpc/syscoinwallet/assetallocationsend/ --- assetallocationsend asset_guid "address_sender" "address_receiver" amount Send an asset allocation you own to another address. Arguments: 1. asset_guid (numeric, required) The asset guid 2. address_sender (string, required) The address to send the allocation from 3. address_receiver (string, required) The address to send the allocation to 4. amount (numeric or string, required) The quantity of asset to send Result: { "hex": "hexstring" (string) the unsigned transaction hexstring. } Examples: > syscoin-cli assetallocationsend "assetguid" "addressfrom" "address" "amount" > curl --user myusername --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id":"curltest", "method": "assetallocationsend", "params": ["assetguid", "addressfrom", "address", "amount"] }' -H 'content-type: text/plain;' http://127.0.0.1:8370/
