--- name: syscoinburntoassetallocation btcversion: 4.2.2 btcgroup: syscoinwallet permalink: en/doc/4.2.2/rpc/syscoinwallet/syscoinburntoassetallocation/ --- syscoinburntoassetallocation "asset_guid" amount Burns Syscoin to the SYSX asset Arguments: 1. asset_guid (string, required) Asset guid of SYSX 2. amount (numeric or string, required) Amount of SYS to burn.
