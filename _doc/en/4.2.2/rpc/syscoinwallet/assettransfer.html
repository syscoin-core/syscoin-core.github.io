--- name: assettransfer btcversion: 4.2.2 btcgroup: syscoinwallet permalink: en/doc/4.2.2/rpc/syscoinwallet/assettransfer/ --- assettransfer "asset_guid" "address" Perform a transfer of ownership on an asset you control. Arguments: 1. asset_guid (string, required) Asset guid 2. address (string, required) New owner of asset. Result: { (json object) "txid" : "hex" (string) The transaction id } Examples: > syscoin-cli assettransfer "asset_guid" "address" > curl --user myusername --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id": "curltest", "method": "assettransfer", "params": ["asset_guid", "address"]}' -H 'content-type: text/plain;' http://127.0.0.1:8370/
